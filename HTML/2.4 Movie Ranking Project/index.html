David's Movie Rankings

My favorite movies that I recommend.

The Matrix

This movie was the first thriller that made me really think about and get curious on how society works.
Epic action, great plot, beautiful cinematography.
Plus this is a movie that is one of its kind in the genre, the only thing that kind of came close after I think was Inception.

The Godfather

Awesome movie describing the life of a mafia boss and intermafia play.
Considered a legend in the world of cinematography, I encourage you to find out why ;)).
Has some cool themes and interactions that helped me understand society differently, great and nuanced plot too!

Terminator 2

We're in the age of AI now, so its cool to see how similarities and differences of robots and humans play out in a movie. This is a fun movie to see that!
Plus Arnold is fcking jacked! Being a bodybuilder isn't my goal but he's been an inspiration for me to hit the gym, been hitting it for a couple months. Hoping to extend it longer!
Has some deep philosophical questions that are brought up but you can ignore them if that's not your thing, unlike the other two.
